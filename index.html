Binaural Audio Demos with Spatial Power Map Visualization

All binaural audio demos are decoded from the ground truth and estimated second-order Ambisonics with different encoding methods (see the paper for details), using the KEMAR HRTF dataset[1]. The spatial power map in the video is generated by processing the Ambisonics signals with the SPARTA PowerMap plugin[2] under the "PWD" mode. Headphones are recommended for the intended spatial audio experience.
